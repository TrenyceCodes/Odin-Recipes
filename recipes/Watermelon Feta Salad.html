Watermelon Feta Salad

Description

This is a picture of the Watermelon Feta Salad. This recipe will be discussed in this section

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Steps/Directions

  1. Whisk the olive oil, vinegar and salt together in a large mixing bowl
  2. Add tomatoes, arugula, and red onion; toss to coat it in the dressing
  3. Gently sir in the watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
